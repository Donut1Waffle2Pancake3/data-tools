Why use this tool

Why Remove Columns from CSV Files?

Removing columns from a CSV helps you keep only the data you need. Whether you are preparing a smaller import, stripping internal fields before sharing, or cleaning exports for other tools, a column remover saves time and keeps formatting valid.

Remove sensitive or internal fields

Clean exports for tools and scripts
