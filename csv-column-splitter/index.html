How it works

How to split a CSV by columns

Upload your CSV, choose which columns to split, and download two separate CSV files. Everything runs in your browser with no uploads or data leaving your device.

1

Upload or paste your CSV file

Paste your CSV data or upload a file. The first row should contain your column headers.

2

Choose columns to split into a new CSV

Select the columns you want in one output file. The remaining columns will automatically go into the second CSV.

3

Download both CSV files

Split the data and download each output as a separate CSV, or copy them to your clipboard.
