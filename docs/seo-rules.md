# SEO Rules

Standards for tool pages (FAQ, How To, headers).

---

## Core

- Solve **real user problems**, not definitions.
- Be **concise, direct, outcome-focused**.  
- Avoid filler, repetition, academic tone.

---

## FAQ

### Principles

- Target **high-intent queries** (errors, “how to fix,” edge cases).
- Reject textbook queries (“what is JSON”).

### Include

- Problems: “why not working?”  
- Specific how-to  
- Edge cases (large files, invalid input)  
- Trust (data/privacy)

### Avoid

- Definitions  
- Generic (“how to use this tool”)  
- Awkward SEO phrasing

### Answers

- 1–3 short paragraphs  
- Immediate, actionable  
- Use bullets for issues  
- No fluff or restating question

---

## How To (formerly “How It Works”)

### Structure

- H2 = **“How to + keyword”**  
- Exactly **3 steps**  
- Pattern: **Input → Process → Output**

### Rules

- Verb-based steps (“Paste JSON”, “Convert…”)  
- Focus on outcome, not UI  
- No technical details or edge cases  
- Title: 3–8 words; description: 1 sentence  

### Template

**How to [keyword]**

Paste your [input], [action], and [result]. Runs in your browser.

1. Paste [input]  
2. Perform action  
3. Download/copy result  

---

## Tone

- Practical, neutral  
- No hype, emojis, or marketing language  

---

## Keywords

- Include primary keyword in:
  - H1  
  - first paragraph  
- Use natural variations in FAQ  
- Do not stuff or repeat excessively  

---

## Header / SEO Structure

### H1

- Match search intent exactly  
- 3–6 words (max ~8)  
- Verb + object  
- No modifiers (free, best, etc.)

### Meta Title

- Format: **keyword - modifier**  
- ~50–60 chars  
- Keyword first  
- Use: Free, Online, Tool, Converter, Viewer  

### Subheading

- 1–2 sentences  
- State outcome + speed + no uploads  

Template:  
Paste your [input], [action], and [result]. Runs in your browser with no uploads.

---

## Page Structure

- H1  
- Subheading  
- Tool UI  
- No long intros or feature lists  

---

## Keyword Placement

- H1: exact  
- Subheading: once  
- “How to” H2: once  
- FAQ: 1–2 variations  

Avoid repetition.

---

## Consistency

Every page:

- H1 = literal tool  
- Title = keyword + modifier  
- Subheading = outcome + trust  

---

## Final Check

- No definition-style FAQs  
- ≥50% problem-based FAQs  
- Answers are concise + actionable  
- Matches real search intent  

---

## What Matters

- Exact intent match  
- 3-step “How to”  
- Clear outcome  
- No fluff  
- Consistent structure  

This site wins by **solving problems quickly**, not teaching concepts.
