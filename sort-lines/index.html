How it works

How to Sort Lines

Everything runs in your browser. No account, no software, no data leaving your device.

1

Paste your text

Paste text into the input box—one item per line. Lists, keywords, or any line-separated content work.

2

Choose order and options

Select ascending or descending, and optionally enable case-sensitive or numeric sort and remove empty lines.

3

Click Sort lines

The tool sorts in the browser and shows the result in the preview area.

4

Copy the output

Copy the sorted text to your clipboard for use in docs, code, or other tools.

Why use this tool

Why Sort Lines?

Unsorted lists are hard to scan and merge. Sorting lines alphabetically or numerically keeps data organized without spreadsheets or scripts.

Useful when you need to:

Order lists quickly

Sort names, tags, URLs, or any line-based list in ascending or descending order.

Numeric sort

Enable numeric sort so numbers order correctly (e.g. 2 before 10) instead of alphabetically.

No spreadsheets needed

Sort plain text in the browser without importing into Excel or writing a script.

Case-sensitive option

Sort with or without respecting letter case, depending on your data.

Privacy
